About PennCycle

PennCycle was founded in 2011 by five students looking to change transportation at Penn. With this common goal, they created a bike share for the Penn community. PennCycle is part of the international movement to make biking as common and accepted as driving and taking public transportation. By riding PennCycle, students are able to save money, get around faster, get exercise, and be more environmentally friendly.

Quaker Corner

Quaker corner is where you can pick up your free helmet, pay for plans in cash, and pick up bike maps. Quaker Corner is open 10 am - 6 pm weekdays and is located in Williams hall, on the Houston Hall side.
