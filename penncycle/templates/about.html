About PennCycle

PennCycle was founded in 2011 by five students looking to change transportation at Penn. With this common goal, they created a bike share for the Penn community. Opening prior to even New York City’s Citi Bike, PennCycle is part of the international movement to make biking as common and accepted as driving and taking public transportation. By riding PennCycle, students are able to save money, get around faster, get exercise, and be more environmentally friendly.

After being funded by Green Campus Partnership’s Greenfund Grant, PennCycle was taken in by Penn Student Agencies. Penn Student Agencies is a group of student run agencies that give students real-life experiences in business, sales, customer service, and management. It provides students with a safe and supportive community to take risks, build partnerships, and run successful businesses.
