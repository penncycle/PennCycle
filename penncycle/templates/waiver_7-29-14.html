ABILITY TO OPERATE A BICYCLE SAFELY

I hereby represent that I am a competent bicycle operator, and confirm that I am sufficiently fit to safely operate a program bicycle and I have no medical conditions that would interfere with my ability to use a program bicycle.

ACKNOWLEDGEMENT REGARDING SAFETY EQUIPMENT

I acknowledge that I have read the provided safe operating procedures and/or viewed the provided safe operating video.

I understand and agree that I am assuming the risk of negligence on the part of the University of Pennsylvania and its employees, agents, and representatives in connection with my use of program bicycles, including the University of Pennsylvania’s negligence in the administration of the program and its maintenance of program bicycles, and confirm that I am not relying on the University of Pennsylvania to safeguard or protect me from the risks, dangers and hazards of using the program bicycle.

I am responsible for any damage to the program bicycle or bicycle parts and accessories included with the bicycle during my use. I am financially responsible for replacing or repairing the bicycle and any of its components upon bending, breaking, ripping, or theft during my rental. (See Appendix A for Costs of Bike Parts)

OTHER TERMS OF USE OF THE BICYCLE

In consideration of PennCycle allowing me to use a program bicycle, and for other good and valuable consideration, the receipt and sufficiency of which is acknowledged, I hereby further agree as follows:

a. I agree not to ride the program bicycle if I notice any mechanical or other problem or safety issue, and I agree to promptly notify PennCycle of all problems and issues and to use a different program bicycle. I further agree to check in any bicycle in need of repair and to notify PennCycle of my assessment of any and all repairs that the bicycle needs at check in.

b. I will not use any cellular telephone, text messaging device, portable music player, or other device that may distract me from safely operating a program bicycle.

c. I will not operate a program bicycle while under the influence of any alcohol, drugs, or other substance that may impair my ability to safely operate a program bicycle.

d. I will not carry a second person on a program bicycle.

e. I will obey any applicable federal, state, or local law, including those for bicycle riders.

f. I will not operate a program bicycle in extreme weather conditions. PennCycle will have the right to refuse use of program bicycles in the event of extreme weather.

g. I acknowledge that I will have the option of reserving a bicycle through a smartphone application (“APP”). If I elect to reserve a bicycle via the “APP” I agree to not share any information specific to the reserved bicycle with any person and to keep such information confidential.

h.Wearing an approved helmet, properly sized, fitted, and fastened, while cycling may protect against an injury, or may lessen the severity of an injury, caused by an impact to the head; however, bicycle helmets are not 100% effective, do not protect against all head injuries, and do not protect against other injuries. Although some state and local laws do not require bicycle riders to wear helmets, PennCycle requires that all riders wear an approved helmet that has been properly sized, fitted, and fastened, according to the manufacturer’s instructions. PennCycle does not represent or warrant the quality or safety characteristics of any helmet, and I agree that PennCycle must not be held liable for any injury I suffer while using a program bicycle, whether or not I am wearing a helmet at the time of injury.

i. I acknowledge that PennCycle has provided for an approved helmet and I have elected to provide a helmet of my own choosing.
