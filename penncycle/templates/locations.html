PennCycle is happy to announce the opening of checkout locations in Rodin, Fisher-Hassenfeld and Ware!


Houston Hall

3417 Spruce Street

Hours: 9 am to 9:30 pm, 7 days a week

Bikes can be checked out at the info desk on the first floor. If there is no one there, please speak to the manager in room 119, directly behind you when you are facing the desk. The manager should be able to check out a bike to you.

Stouffer College House

3702 Spruce Street (above Wawa)

Hours:

  • 9:45 am - 4 pm and 6 pm - 11 pm Monday
  • 9:45 am - 5:30 pm and 6 pm - 11 pm Tuesday through Thursday
  • 9:45 am - 5:30 pm and 6 pm - 9 pm Friday
  • 11am to 11pm Saturday and Sunday

Bikes are stored in the Stouffer courtyard up the stairs. To check-out or return a bike, go into the D building and to the right.

Weekend hours to be announced.

Penn Student Agencies Store

117 Williams Hall (across from Houston Hall)

Hours: 10 am to 6 pm Monday through Friday
